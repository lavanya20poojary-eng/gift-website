💌 A Letter For You

Dear Ruchi, ❤️

From that random conversation we had back in 2021, I never imagined it would turn into something this beautiful. I never thought that one simple discussion would give me a person who would become my safe place, my comfort, and my home away from home. You've been so much more than just a friend. You've been my sister, my parent whenever I needed guidance, my teacher who never hesitated to scold me when I was wrong, my biggest supporter, and the person who always knew exactly how to handle me. You understand me in ways that very few people ever could. The truth is, you've been one of the best parts of my life. Everyone might think I'm okay because I'm not showing how much this hurts, but deep inside, I'm really not. It feels like a part of me is moving away. That's exactly what this feels like—like I'm losing a piece of my everyday life. Knowing that I won't get to see you whenever I want or annoy you whenever I feel like it is something I'm still trying to accept. At the same time, I'm genuinely so happy for you. You're moving to Mysuru to chase your dreams, to grow, and to build the future you deserve. Watching you grow makes me proud, even if it means I have to miss you from a distance. You deserve every opportunity that comes your way, and I'll always be the loudest person cheering for you. But I have one selfish request... Promise me that no matter how many new people you meet, no matter where life takes you, you'll never replace my place with someone else. I know it's selfish, but I can't help it. What we have is too special to me, and I want our bond to stay exactly that—ours. Distance may change how often we meet, but it will never change what you mean to me. No number of kilometers can replace the memories we've made, the late-night conversations, the laughter, the fights, the endless teasing, the advice, or the comfort of simply knowing you're there. Thank you for standing by me through every version of myself. Thank you for believing in me, for correcting me when I needed it, for celebrating my victories, and for holding me together during my worst days. Thank you for loving me even when I wasn't easy to deal with. I already miss you more than words can explain, and I know I'm going to miss you even more once you leave. But no matter where life takes us, you'll always have a permanent place in my heart. Go make Mysuru proud. Go chase every dream you've ever had. Just don't forget that you'll always have someone back here who loves you endlessly, believes in you, and will always be waiting to hear about every little thing happening in your life. I love you so much, Ruchi. More than I probably say, and definitely more than you'll ever know. No matter where you go, you'll always be my home away from home. ❤️ And there's one more thing I'm going to hold on to— I'm going to wait for the day you come back. Maybe life will take us in different directions for a while, but I'll always look forward to the moment we're together again. I can't wait for the days when we'll laugh like nothing ever changed, create new memories, annoy each other all over again, and live together just like we've always imagined. No matter how long it takes, I'll always keep that hope alive because some bonds are simply meant to last forever. ❤️
